Exhibit 10.17

Pursuant to an oral arrangement between Imperial Holdings, LLC and Warburg 
Investment Corporation (Warburg), Mr. Tony Mitchell serves as the chief 
executive officer of Imperial Holdings, LLC and Imperial Holdings, LLC 
provides Warburg with (i) office space; (ii) office equipment; and (iii) 
personnel. Imperial Holdings, LLC pays Warburg for Mr. Mitchells service 
and Mr. Mitchell is paid by Warburg. Imperial Holdings, LLC has entered 
into a written employment agreement with Mr. Mitchell that will become 
effective upon the closing of this offering. At that time, the oral 
arrangement with Warburg will terminate.
